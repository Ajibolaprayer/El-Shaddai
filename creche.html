Aims of our Crèche

  1. Provide a Safe and Secure Environment
    • Ensure children are protected from harm and cared for in a safe, clean, and healthy setting.
  2. Promote Holistic Development
    • Support children's physical, cognitive, emotional, social, and language development through age-appropriate activities.
  3. Meet Children's Basic Needs
    • Provide proper nutrition, hygiene, rest, and healthcare to promote healthy growth.
  4. Encourage Learning Through Play
    • Offer stimulating play experiences that foster creativity, curiosity, problem-solving, and early learning skills.
  5. Develop Social Skills
    • Help children learn to interact positively with others by sharing, cooperating, communicating, and respecting others.
  6. Support Emotional Well-being
    • Build children's confidence, self-esteem, and independence in a caring and supportive environment.
  7. Prepare Children for Preschool and Formal Education
    • Introduce routines, basic literacy and numeracy concepts, and learning habits that ease the transition to preschool or primary school.
  8. Support Working Parents and Guardians
    • Provide reliable childcare services that enable parents to work, study, or attend to other responsibilities with peace of mind.
  9. Promote Good Health and Hygiene Practices
    • Teach children healthy habits such as handwashing, personal hygiene, and healthy eating.
  10. Foster Positive Partnerships with Families
    • Work closely with parents and guardians to support each child's individual needs and development.

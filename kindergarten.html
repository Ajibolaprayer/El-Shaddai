Aim of our Kindergarten

  1. Promote Holistic Development
    • Foster children's cognitive, physical, emotional, social, and creative growth through age-appropriate activities.
  2. Prepare Children for Primary Education
    • Develop the foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, communication, and critical thinking needed for a successful transition to primary school.
  3. Encourage Learning Through Play
    • Use play-based learning to stimulate curiosity, creativity, imagination, and problem-solving skills.
  4. Develop Social and Emotional Skills
    • Help children learn cooperation, sharing, respect, empathy, self-confidence, and positive relationships with peers and adults.
  5. Enhance Language and Communication Skills
    • Improve listening, speaking, reading readiness, vocabulary, and early writing abilities.
  6. Promote Physical Development
    • Strengthen both fine and gross motor skills through indoor and outdoor activities, games, and creative tasks.

The overall aim of our kindergarten is to nurture children's all-round development and equip them with the knowledge, skills, attitudes, and confidence they need for lifelong learning and a successful start in formal education.

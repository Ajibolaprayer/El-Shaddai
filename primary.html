Aim of our Primary School

  1. Provide Basic Education
    • Equip pupils with fundamental literacy, numeracy, and communication skills essential for everyday life and future learning.
  2. Promote Holistic Development
    • Foster the intellectual, physical, emotional, social, moral, and creative development of every child.
  3. Develop Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills
    • Encourage pupils to think independently, ask questions, analyze information, and solve problems effectively.
  4. Instill Good Moral Values
    • Promote honesty, respect, discipline, responsibility, kindness, tolerance, and integrity.
  5. Encourage Effective Communication
    • Develop pupils' ability to listen, speak, read, and write confidently and effectively.
  6. Promote Healthy Living
    • Teach good personal hygiene, healthy habits, physical fitness, and environmental awareness.
  7. Develop Social and Citizenship Skills
    • Help pupils learn cooperation, teamwork, leadership, respect for diversity, and responsible citizenship.
  8. Discover and Develop Talents
    • Provide opportunities for pupils to explore and develop their talents in academics, sports, arts, music, and other extracurricular activities.
  9. Prepare Pupils for Secondary Education
    • Build the academic knowledge, study habits, confidence, and skills needed for success in secondary school.
  10. Promote Lifelong Learning
    • Cultivate curiosity, creativity, self-confidence, and a positive attitude toward learning that continues throughout life.

The primary aim of our primary school is to provide quality basic education that develops children's knowledge, skills, character, and values, preparing them to become confident learners, responsible citizens, and productive members of society.
